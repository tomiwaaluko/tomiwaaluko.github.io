Introduction

My name is Tomiwa Aluko and I am currently a senior pursing a B.S. in Computer Engineering with a minor in Technology Entrepreneurship at the University of Central Florida. I am a Nigerian-American from Miami, Florida, where my household consists of my mother, father, and younger brother.

I am a proud brother of the Alpha Phi Alpha Fraternity, Incorporated where I was initiated at the Xi Iota Chapter at the University of Central Florida. I currently serve as the East Area Assistant Director and have held chapter positions such as President, Secretary, and Historian.

I’m also involved in other organizations on campus such as the National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE), the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE), Hack @ UCF, and UCF Black Student Union (BSU). I currently serve as the Senator for NSBE.
